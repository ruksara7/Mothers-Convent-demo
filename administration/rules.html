School Rules

General Rules

  • Students must come to school punctually and in proper uniform.
  • Respect should be shown to teachers, staff, and fellow students at all times.
  • Use of mobile phones is not allowed inside the classroom without permission.
  • Bringing or using any kind of tobacco, alcohol, or drugs is strictly prohibited.
  • Students must maintain cleanliness in classrooms, corridors, and playgrounds.

Attendance & Discipline

  • Regular attendance is compulsory; leave must be formally informed in case of absence.
  • Repeated late‑coming or absenteeism may lead to a written warning or parent‑teacher meeting.
  • Any act of cheating, aggression, or bullying will be dealt with strictly.
  • Students are expected to follow the instructions of teachers and staff without argument.

Homework & Examinations

  • Homework must be completed neatly and submitted on the given date.
  • Copying or helping others to copy in examinations is a serious offence.
  • Use of unfair means in tests or exams will result in cancellation of marks.
