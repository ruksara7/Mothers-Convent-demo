About Mothers Convent School

Mothers Convent School, located in Varanasi, is dedicated to providing quality education in a nurturing and disciplined environment. Our goal is to develop academic excellence, strong moral values, and overall personality growth in every student.

The school focuses on modern teaching methods, supportive faculty, and balanced development through academics, sports, and co-curricular activities.
